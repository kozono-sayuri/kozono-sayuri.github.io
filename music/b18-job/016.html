📖 Overview｜這首歌想說什麼？

取材自 《《約伯記》2:7–8 ，在一切失去之後，連身體也開始崩壞。 沒有雷霆、沒有質問，只剩一個人坐在灰燼裡，用陶片刮著自己的傷。　 “I scrape my skin,” inspired by Job 2:7–8, portrays a man who has lost everything, now facing the collapse of his own body. No thunder, no protest—only a figure in ashes, scraping his wounds with a broken shard.

這首歌以〈多娜多娜〉的民謠氣質為靈感，用極簡的旋律與克制的人聲， 描寫一種「連痛都不願承認」的狀態。當苦難接連而來，人不再掙扎，而是只剩呼吸。 如果你也曾在某個時刻，無法言語，只能靜靜撐過——也許這首歌，會陪你坐一會。　 Drawing from the folk spirit of “Donna Donna,” this piece uses minimal melody and restrained vocals to express a state where even pain is no longer spoken. When suffering comes again and again, resistance fades—only breath remains. If you have ever endured something in silence, perhaps this song will sit with you for a while.
